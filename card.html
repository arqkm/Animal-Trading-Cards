Swan

They can sleep standing on one leg or whilst floating, usually with their heads tucked back under a wing.

  • Scientific Name: Cygnus
  • Food: Small Fish, Frogs
  • Average Lifespan: 12-30 years
  • Habitat: Shallow ponds

Swans are the largest extant members of the waterfowl family Anatidae, and are among the largest flying birds.
